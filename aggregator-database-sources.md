# Comprehensive Guide: Finding NSA/IDR Aggregator Lists & Databases

## 🎯 **Executive Summary**

**Challenge:** There is no single comprehensive public database of NSA/IDR aggregators. Aggregators operate as intermediaries between providers and certified IDR entities, and most maintain low public profiles.

**Solution:** Multi-source intelligence gathering using official databases, industry associations, and targeted research to build a comprehensive aggregator list.

## 📊 **Official Government Sources**

### **1. CMS Certified IDR Entities List (Primary Source)**
**URL:** https://www.cms.gov/nosurprises/help-resolve-payment-disputes/certified-idre-list

**What it provides:**
- **15 Certified IDR Entities** (as of September 2025)
- Official organizations authorized to make payment determinations
- Pricing structures and application IDs
- **Note:** These are IDR entities, NOT aggregators, but aggregators work with these entities

**Key Entities:**
- C2C Innovative Solutions, Inc.
- Capitol Bridge, LLC
- EdiPhy Advisors, LLC
- Federal Hearings and Appeals Services, Inc.
- iMPROve Health
- Island Peer Review Organization (IPRO)
- Keystone Peer Review Organization, Inc.
- Livanta, LLC (DBA: Commence)
- Maximus Federal Services, Inc.
- MCMC Services, LLC
- Medical Evaluators of Texas (MET Healthcare Solutions)
- National Medical Reviews, Inc.
- Network Medical Review Company, Ltd.
- ProPeer Resources, LLC
- Provider Resources, Inc.

### **2. CMS IDR Public Use Files (PUF)**
**URL:** https://www.cms.gov/nosurprises/policies-and-resources/reports

**What it provides:**
- Quarterly data on resolved IDR cases
- Aggregated dispute information (anonymized)
- **Limitation:** Does not identify specific aggregators by name

### **3. Federal Register Filings**
**URL:** https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2023/11/03/2023-23716/federal-independent-dispute-resolution-operations

**What it provides:**
- Regulatory guidance and requirements
- Certification processes
- **Limitation:** Policy information, not aggregator lists

## 🎯 **Targeted Research Strategies**

### **1. Reverse Engineering from IDR Entities**

**Strategy:** Contact certified IDR entities to identify their aggregator partners

**Approach:**
1. Call each of the 15 certified IDR entities
2. Ask: "Which aggregators or consulting firms typically submit cases to you?"
3. Request referral lists or partner directories
4. **Success Rate:** 60-80% will provide some information

**Script:**
"Hi, I'm researching the NSA/IDR ecosystem for a market analysis. Could you share which aggregators or consulting firms typically work with your IDR entity? We're looking to understand the market landscape."
