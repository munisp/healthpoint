# Georgetown Enhanced IDR Platform: End-to-End Aggregator Evolution Strategy

## 🎯 **Executive Summary**

The Georgetown Enhanced IDR Platform is positioned to evolve from a **processing and analytics platform** into a **comprehensive end-to-end aggregator** that directly interfaces with providers and delivers complete solutions to CMS. This transformation would eliminate intermediaries and create unprecedented value across the entire NSA/IDR ecosystem.

---

## 📊 **Current State: 3 Core Feeder Data Sources**

### **1. Provider Systems (EHR/Billing)**
**Source:** Healthcare provider electronic health records and billing systems  
**Data Type:** Clinical and financial transaction data  
**Current Flow:** Provider → Aggregator → Platform

#### **Provider Data Components:**
```
Clinical Data:
- Patient demographics and insurance verification
- Service codes (CPT, HCPCS, ICD-10)
- Provider NPI and taxonomy codes
- Service dates and locations
- Clinical documentation and notes

Financial Data:
- Billed charges and fee schedules
- Insurance eligibility and benefits
- Prior authorization status
- Payment history and adjustments
- Network participation status
```

### **2. Payer Systems (Claims Processing)**
**Source:** Health insurance payer claims processing and adjudication systems  
**Data Type:** Coverage determination and payment processing data  
**Current Flow:** Payer → Aggregator → Platform

#### **Payer Data Components:**
```
Coverage Data:
- Member eligibility and benefits
- Network provider directories
- Coverage policies and guidelines
- Prior authorization requirements
- Exclusions and limitations

Claims Data:
- Claims adjudication decisions
- Payment amounts and denials
- Explanation of benefits (EOB)
- Appeal and grievance history
- Quality metrics and outcomes
```

### **3. Regulatory/Reference Systems (CMS/Federal)**
**Source:** CMS databases, federal registries, and regulatory systems  
**Data Type:** Compliance requirements and reference data  
**Current Flow:** Federal Systems → Aggregator → Platform

#### **Regulatory Data Components:**
```
Compliance Data:
- QPA calculation methodologies
- Geographic fee schedules
- Network adequacy requirements
- NSA compliance guidelines
- IDR entity certifications

Reference Data:
- Provider enrollment and sanctions
- Quality reporting requirements
- Medicare fee schedules
- Geographic adjustment factors
- Specialty taxonomy codes
```
