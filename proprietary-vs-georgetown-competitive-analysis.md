## Executive Summary

This analysis provides a detailed head-to-head comparison of HealthPoint's Proprietary Intelligence (HPI) approach versus Georgetown University's methodology across all competitive dimensions. ### **Georgetown University Methodology**
- **Foundation:** Academic research analysis of 586,581 federal IDR cases
- **Approach:** Descriptive statistical analysis with peer-reviewed methodology

- **Update Cycle:** Quarterly/annual academic publication schedule
- **Scope:** Retrospective analysis of completed IDR cases

- **Foundation:** Multi-source fusion intelligence with real-time market data

- **Data Source:** Real-time market intelligence + federal data + proprietary sources
- **Update Cycle:** Real-time continuous learning and adaptation
- **Scope:** Predictive intelligence with strategic optimization
