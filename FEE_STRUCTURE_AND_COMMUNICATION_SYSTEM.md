## **NSA/IDR Platform: Comprehensive Fee Structure & Communication System**

### **1. Introduction**

This document outlines the comprehensive fee structure and transparent communication system implemented in the NSA/IDR Healthcare Claims Platform. The platform is designed to provide complete transparency in billing and transaction costs for aggregators, ensuring no hidden fees and clear, predictable pricing.

### **2. Aggregator Billing Plans**

The platform offers four distinct billing plans for aggregators, each with a different set of features, provider limits, and pricing:

| Plan Name | Monthly Cost | Max Providers | Per Dispute Fee | Included Transactions |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Standard Plan** | $299/month | 25 | $15 | 50 |
| **Premium Plan** | $599/month | 50 | $12 | 100 |
| **Enterprise Plan** | $1,299/month | Unlimited | $8 | 500 |
| **NSA/IDR Pro Plan** | $899/month | 75 | $10 | 200 |

### **3. Transaction Fee Structure**

To cover the operational costs of processing payments and refunds, the platform applies transaction fees based on the payment method used. These fees are transparently communicated to aggregators through multiple channels.

| Payment Method | Fee | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **ACH Transfer** | $0.50 | Standard bank transfer |
| **Same-Day ACH** | $1.25 | Expedited bank transfer |
| **Wire Transfer** | $20.00 | Secure wire transfer |
| **Credit Card** | 3.2% + $0.50 | Credit card processing |
| **Check** | $2.75 | Physical check printing/mailing |

### **4. Volume Discounts**

To incentivize high-volume usage, the platform offers volume discounts on transaction fees for transactions that exceed the plan's included allowance:

- **10-30% discounts** based on plan and transaction volume
- Discounts are automatically applied to monthly invoices

### **5. Communication & Transparency**

The platform ensures complete transparency in billing and transaction costs through a multi-faceted communication system:

**a. Fee Calculator**
- An interactive tool that allows aggregators to estimate their monthly costs based on their expected usage patterns.
- Provides a detailed breakdown of all fee components, including monthly subscription, dispute processing fees, transaction fees, and taxes.

**b. Pricing Plans Interface**
- A comprehensive comparison of all billing plans, showing features, pricing, and included transaction allowances.
- Allows aggregators to make informed decisions about the best plan for their needs.

**c. Transaction Fee Structure Display**
- A clear and concise breakdown of all transaction fees for each payment method.
- Ensures aggregators understand the costs associated with each payment type.

**d. Real-time Invoicing**
- Detailed monthly invoices with line-item breakdowns of all charges.
- Provides a complete audit trail of all platform usage and associated costs.

**e. Support System**
- Multiple channels for billing questions, including email, phone, and live chat.
- A comprehensive FAQ section that answers common questions about fees and billing.

### **6. Conclusion**

The NSA/IDR Healthcare Claims Platform provides a complete and transparent fee structure and communication system that ensures aggregators have a clear understanding of all costs associated with using the platform. By providing multiple communication channels and real-time cost estimation tools, the platform empowers aggregators to make informed decisions and manage their costs effectively.
